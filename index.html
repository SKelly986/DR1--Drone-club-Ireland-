DR1 - Drone Club Ireland.

Welcome to DR1, the Drone Club of Ireland. Whether you're a guest or a member, we are delighted to have you with us.


At DR1, we organize a variety of activities throughout the year for our members to enjoy. These activities include drone racing competitions, aerial photography contests, and educational workshops on drone technology and safety. We also regularly organize group flights where members can explore some of the most beautiful and scenic locations around Ireland.
